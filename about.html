--- title: "About Paul" permalink: /about/ ---

Paul Bogard is the Lead Engineer for KC-46 Aircrew Training Systems with the United States Air Force at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio. He has been performing acquisition of full flight simulators as a systems engineer since 2009. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology and a Master of Science in Computer Engineering from Wright State University

Paul enjoys travel, airports, airplanes, trains, interesting roads and intersections, maps, and nearly anything else related to transportation. He currently lives in Beavercreek, Ohio.
